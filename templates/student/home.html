Case Filing Guidelines

Date:

Please specify date on which the incident occurred or submitting the complaint.

Time of Incident:

Specify the time the incident occurred.

Location:

Provide the location where the incident occurred or the context of the report. Include any relevant details such as school building, specific place which the incident occurred.

Party Involved:

Input the specific individual in the incident or report. This may include students and faculty.

Description:

Please provide a detailed description of the incident or issue. Include all relevant information, such as specific actions, conversations, or circumstances, surrounding the complaint. Be as specific and clear as possible.

Any member of the PUP community has the ability to report complaints they believe violate our Community Guidelines. When a complaint is flagged, it doesn't automatically result in immediate action or sanctions. Flagged complaints are reviewed in accordance with the following guidelines:

Complaints that violate our School Guidelines and Student Code of Conduct are subject to sanctions. Actions deemed inappropriate for the entire campus community may result in disciplinary measures.
