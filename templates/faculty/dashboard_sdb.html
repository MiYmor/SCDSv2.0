Only PUP faculty members have the authority to submit violations that they believe violate our Community Guidelines. When a violation report is submitted, it doesn't automatically result in immediate action or sanctions. Each reported violation is reviewed in accordance with the following guidelines:

Violations that contravene our School Guidelines and Student Code of Conduct are subject to sanctions. Actions deemed inappropriate for the entire campus community may result in disciplinary measures.

Furthermore, PUP faculty members play a crucial role as investigators in the review of cases or complaints, meticulously examining each matter and proposing appropriate sanctions in strict adherence to institutional regulations.
